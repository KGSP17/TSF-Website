The Spirito Foundation exists to uplift and connect generations through service, creativity, and compassion. Our mission is to bring visibility, dignity, and joy to older adults while empowering student leaders to make meaningful impact in their communities.

Who We Are

The Spirito Foundation is composed of passionate student leaders committed to improving the lives of older adults through creative programming, intergenerational engagement, and local community support.

Why We Care

We believe every generation has something to offer—and something to gain. Our programs aim to bridge gaps, reduce isolation, and celebrate stories through meaningful connection.

Divisions

The Spirito Foundation proudly supports student-led divisions at universities across the nation. These divisions carry out our mission by organizing service initiatives, engaging with local retirement communities, and fostering student leadership.

  • Tide4Tuscaloosa – University of Alabama: Our founding division focused on intergenerational connection through performance and engagement.
  • More coming soon! Interested in starting a division at your university? Contact us here.
